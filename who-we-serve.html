Defense

For over two decades, Advance Manufacturing Technology has been a crucial cog in the complex infrastructure that compromises federal defense manufacturing and contracting. Our firm has delivered reliable, precise, innovating machining solutions to many of the government's thorniest defense technology mandates. We are proud of our role in US defense contracting and enjoy strong relationships with all the major US defense contractors.

Aerospace

What kind of aerospace manufacturers partner with Advance Manufacturing Technology? Those whose programs work in unique, mission-critical, and whose products must be fail-safe. Mining, Oil & Gas

As Manufacturing diversifies, heavy industry operations like mining, firearms, robotics, communications, instrumentation, and oil & gas processing plants are in need of high-end machining. AMT has created customized parts and equiment for some of the largest industrial mines and processing plants in the United States. Our work in heavy industry has also led to multiple projects for other commercial enterprises, from firearms parts and accessories to automotive parts and more.
