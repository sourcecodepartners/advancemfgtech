manufacturing-technology

In 1976, Keith Barney founded Advance Manufactuing Technology in the family garage. Since then, we've grown to be a leader in precision-machined components, but we still base ou business on the principles that led to our original success: technology, process, and customet focus.

Our vision is a future where Us manufacturers in aeonautics, defense, medical, and other important industries have access to nimble, flexible, world-class machining. We exist to make that happen.
