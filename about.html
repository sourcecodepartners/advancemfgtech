About Advance

manufacturing-technology

In 1976, Keith Barney founded Advance Manufactuing Technology in the family garage. Since then, we've grown to be a leader in precision-machined components, but we still base our business on the principles that led to our original success: technology, process, and customer focus.

Our vision is a future where US manufacturers in aerospace, defense, medical, and other important industries have access to nimble, flexible, world-class machining. We exist to make that happen.

About Our Technology

Technology is so central to our success that it's even in our name. Beginning with our installation of the first CNC mill in the Salt Valley, we've always leveraged cutting-edge technology to provide high quality with effciency. Today our horizontal mills, high-speed milling machines, 3-turret lathes, and innovative tooling allow us to drill, mill and turn with efficieny and expertise. The result? Competitive costs and as promised on-time delivery.

our process

Our core processes are the foundation for our impressive record of producing even difficult parts right the first time. We are ISO9001 and AS9100 certified, and we use advanced quality management, knowledge management and continuous improvement systems and techniques to help achieve better than 99.5 percent quality conformance.

our people

Our people are the very heart of our business. None of the work we do would be possible without our amazing team executing each day to ensure things get done. Our operations, activities and our success all rely on the people who work for us.
