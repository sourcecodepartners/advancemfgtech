CNC Machinist

We have an opening for a CNC Machinist. Thorouh knowledge of G&M codes is required as well as knowledge of modern CNC machining centers (Our Shop runs all Mori-Seiki machining centers), including live tooling, Y-axis, multi-turret, and sub-spindle equipped turning centers. The qualified candidate will be responsible for setup, operating, in-process inspection, basic troubleshooting and documentation of processes and results as required by AS9100.

We have a progressive state-of-the-art shop, that is clean with lots of opportunities, cross-training, and is a great place to work, Knowledge of manufacturing, use of precision measuring tools, ability to read blueprints, and safe work habits required. Benefits include Paid Time Off (PTO), paid holidays, a 401k with company matching and Health Insurance.

Requirements for CNC Machinist:
  • Must be able to Set Up Lathes and Mills. Must be familiar with G-Code.
  • GibbsCAM experience desired, but not necessary.
  • We are a Mori-Seki facility.
  • Mamilliarity with quality practices is a must.
  • Able to communicate effectively with employees and management.
  • Must be familliar with all general shop practices, as well as blueprint. GD&T familiarity desired, not required.
  • 3 years min, experience.
  • This os NOT entry-level.
  • Fast-paced environment.
  • Full time, 40 hours or more per week.
  • Minimum Education: High School Diploma.
